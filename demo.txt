Here are the top 10 most visited tourist destinations in India, based on various sources:
Taj Mahal, Agra: This iconic mausoleum is one of the Seven Wonders of the World and attracts millions of visitors annually due to its stunning architecture and historical significance.
Jaipur, Rajasthan: Known as the Pink City, Jaipur is famous for its rich history, magnificent forts, palaces, and vibrant markets. Key attractions include the Amer Fort and City Palace.
Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh: One of the oldest cities in the world, Varanasi is a sacred city for Hindus, renowned for its ghats along the Ganges River and numerous temples.
Goa: Famous for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese heritage, Goa is a popular destination for both domestic and international tourists.
Delhi: The capital city offers a mix of historical landmarks like the Red Fort and India Gate, alongside modern attractions and bustling markets.
Kerala (Backwaters): Known as "God's Own Country," Kerala is famous for its serene backwaters, lush landscapes, and unique houseboat experiences.
Amritsar, Punjab: Home to the Golden Temple, Amritsar is a spiritual center for Sikhs and attracts visitors with its rich culture and history.
Manali, Himachal Pradesh: This picturesque hill station is popular for its scenic beauty, adventure sports, and as a getaway from the summer heat.
Ooty, Tamil Nadu: A charming hill station known for its pleasant climate, tea gardens, and colonial architecture.
Munnar, Kerala: Renowned for its tea plantations and scenic landscapes, Munnar is a popular destination for nature lovers and honeymooners.
